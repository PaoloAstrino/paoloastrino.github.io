Privacy Policy

My Privacy Philosophy

Your privacy is sacred to me. As a data analyst, I understand how valuable and sensitive personal information is. That's why I've built this portfolio with a "privacy-first" mindset:

  • Zero data collection - I don't collect, store, or analyze visitor behavior
  • No tracking whatsoever - No Google Analytics, no Facebook pixels, no hidden trackers
  • No third-party services - Your data never leaves this website
  • Minimal contact data handling - Contact form data is used solely for response purposes

Information We Collect

I collect the absolute minimum information necessary. When you contact me through the contact form, I only collect:

  • Your name and email address (only what you provide)
  • The message content you choose to share
  • Timestamp of your inquiry (for response organization only)

That's it. No IP addresses, no browser fingerprinting, no location tracking, no behavioral analysis.

How I Use Your Information

I use your information for one purpose only: to respond to your inquiry. Specifically:

  • Direct response only - I'll email you back to answer your question or continue our conversation
  • No marketing ever - I will never send you promotional emails, newsletters, or marketing materials
  • No sharing, period - Your information stays with me and is never shared with anyone
  • No database storage - I don't build contact lists or customer databases
  • No automated processing - Your message is read and responded to personally by me

After our conversation ends, your contact information is deleted.

Data Storage & Security

Your data is handled with extreme care and minimal retention:

  • Temporary storage only - Contact form data is held just long enough to respond to you
  • Secure transmission - All form submissions are processed securely
  • No third-party analytics - Zero Google Analytics, Facebook tracking, or any external analytics services
  • No advertising networks - No Google Ads, no retargeting pixels, no ad tracking
  • No social media tracking - Social media buttons don't track you until you click them
  • Local hosting - Website hosted without data-mining hosting providers
  • Immediate deletion - Contact data is deleted after our conversation concludes

I practice what I preach about data privacy.

Your Rights (And My Commitments)

You have complete control over your data:

  • Right to know - Ask me what data I have about you (spoiler: very little, and temporarily)
  • Right to deletion - Request immediate deletion of your contact information at any time
  • Right to portability - Get a copy of any data I have about you
  • Right to withdraw consent - Change your mind about being contacted at any time
  • Right to contact me - Ask any privacy-related questions directly

I will respond to all privacy requests within 24 hours. Because your privacy matters that much to me.

Cookies (The Honest Truth)

I use exactly ONE cookie, and here's why:

  • Theme Preference: Remembers if you prefer dark or light mode (that's it!)
  • Cookie Consent: Remembers your choice about cookies (so I don't keep asking)
  • No tracking cookies - Zero. None. Nada.
  • No analytics cookies - I don't track your behavior, clicks, or time spent
  • No advertising cookies - No ads, no retargeting, no commercial tracking
  • No social media cookies - Social buttons don't track until you click them
  • No performance cookies - No speed tracking or performance monitoring of your usage
